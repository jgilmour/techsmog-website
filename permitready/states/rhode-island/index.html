Rhode Island Permit Test Overview

Minimum permit age in Rhode Island is 16. PermitReady also highlights important Rhode Island licensing rules so you can study the test format and the steps that come after you pass.

  • Question count: 25
  • Passing score: 20 correct (80%)
  • Minimum permit age: 16
  • Time limit: No official time limit listed
  • Split test: No separate scored sections listed

Study Modes

  • Practice Quiz: Untimed questions with explanations after every answer.
  • Practice Test: A state-sized simulation with 25 questions.
  • Study by Category: Focus on road signs, traffic laws, safe driving, parking, right of way, and more.
  • Review Missed Questions: Revisit mistakes quickly and learn why the correct answer is right.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor mastery, streaks, recent scores, and study history.
  • Offline-First: Study without a constant internet connection.

State-Specific Requirements

  • Must hold learner's permit for 6 months before applying for a restricted license
  • 50 hours of supervised driving required (10 at night)
  • During first 12 months of restricted license: no driving between 1 AM and 5 AM
  • During first 12 months of restricted license: no more than one non-family passenger under 21
  • Drivers under 18 prohibited from using any mobile device, including hands-free, while driving

Who This Is For

Great for teens preparing for a first learner's permit, parents helping a new driver study, and anyone retaking the knowledge test in Rhode Island.
