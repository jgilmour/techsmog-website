North Carolina Permit Test Overview

Minimum permit age in North Carolina is 15. PermitReady also highlights important North Carolina licensing rules so you can study the test format and the steps that come after you pass.

  • Question count: 25
  • Passing score: 20 correct (80%)
  • Minimum permit age: 15
  • Time limit: No official time limit listed
  • Split test: Yes, separate scored sections apply

Study Modes

  • Study by Category: Focus on road signs, traffic laws, safe driving, parking, right of way, and more.

  • Offline-First: Study without a constant internet connection.

State-Specific Requirements

  • The test is strictly split: You must pass a 'Regulatory Signs' recognition test (identifying signs by shape/color only) BEFORE you can take the 25-question written knowledge test
  • Level 1 Limited Learner Permit holders can only drive between 5 AM and 9 PM for the first 6 months
  • Under the 'Fender Bender' law, you must move your vehicle to the shoulder if involved in a minor crash with no injuries
  • Must have a 'Driving Eligibility Certificate' from your school to prove you are passing your classes

Who This Is For

Great for teens preparing for a first learner's permit, parents helping a new driver study, and anyone retaking the knowledge test in North Carolina.
