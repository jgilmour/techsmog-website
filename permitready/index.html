Frequently Asked Questions

Which states are supported?

Currently 13 high-volume states: Massachusetts (MA), California (CA), Texas (TX), Florida (FL), New York (NY), Pennsylvania (PA), Illinois (IL), Ohio (OH), North Carolina (NC), Michigan (MI), Georgia (GA), New Jersey (NJ), and Virginia (VA).

Does it work offline?

Yes! All question banks are bundled with the app, so you can study anywhere without an internet connection.

Are the questions similar to the real test?

Yes, our questions are based on official DMV materials and cover all topics tested on your state's permit exam.

What's the difference between Practice Quiz and Practice Test?

Practice Quiz has no time limit and shows explanations after each answer, making it perfect for learning. Practice Test is a timed 30-minute exam simulation without explanations, designed to prepare you for the real testing experience.

Is there a subscription?

No! The app is completely free with optional ads. You can remove ads with a one-time $1.99 purchase‚Äîno subscriptions, no recurring fees.
