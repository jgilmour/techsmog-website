PermitReady

Your guide to passing the driver's permit exam

PermitReady is an educational app designed to help teenagers (ages 15-17) study for their driver's permit exam. With practice questions, progress tracking, and focused study sessions, PermitReady makes learning the rules of the road engaging and effective.

Features

Practice Questions

Study with comprehensive practice questions covering all topics on the permit exam.

Progress Tracking

Track your quiz scores, study streaks, and performance in each category to focus on areas that need improvement.

Offline Access

All your study data is stored locally on your device. Study anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

Privacy-First

No accounts, no tracking, no personal information collected. Your data stays on your device.

Pricing

  • Free version: Full access with ads
  • Ad-free version: $1.99 one-time purchase
  • No subscriptions: Pay once, use forever

Platform

PermitReady is available for iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
