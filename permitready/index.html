The complete study app for teens preparing for their learner's permit exam. Practice with questions based on the latest 2026 state handbooks, track your progress, and pass on your first try.

PermitReady is a free iOS app for teens preparing for their driver's permit (learner's permit) exam. It covers 19 US states — including California DMV, Texas DPS, Florida DHSMV, New York DMV, and Massachusetts RMV — with state-specific practice questions drawn from official 2026 handbooks. Features include an untimed practice quiz, a 30-minute timed test simulation, study by category, offline access, and progress tracking. The app is free with ads; a one-time $4.99 purchase removes ads with no subscriptions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which states are supported?

PermitReady currently supports 19 states: Massachusetts (RMV), California (DMV), Texas (DPS), Florida (DHSMV), New York (DMV), Pennsylvania (PennDOT), Illinois (SOS), Ohio (BMV), North Carolina (NCDMV), Michigan (SOS), Georgia (DDS), New Jersey (MVC), Virginia (DMV), Maryland (MVA), Missouri (DOR), Indiana (BMV), Tennessee (DOSHS), Washington (DOL), and Arizona (MVD). Questions are based on publicly available handbook sources.

Does it work offline?

Yes! All question banks are bundled with the app, so you can study anywhere without an internet connection.

Are the questions similar to the real test?

Yes, our questions are based on official state driver handbooks (DMV, RMV, DPS, BMV, and other licensing agency materials) and cover all topics tested on your state's 2026 permit exam.

What's the difference between Practice Quiz and Practice Test?

Practice Quiz has no time limit and shows explanations after each answer, making it perfect for learning. Practice Test is a timed 30-minute exam simulation without explanations, designed to prepare you for the real testing experience.

Is there a subscription?

No! PermitReady is free to use with ads. You can remove ads with a one-time $4.99 purchase—no subscriptions, no recurring fees.
